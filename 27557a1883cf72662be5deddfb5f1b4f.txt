Character/Description/NexusHunter=NexusHunter is an elite warrior from the nation of Iresia, the most advanced and powerful realm in the Nexus. At least, it was, until division and strife led to such a violent conflict that it tore their nation and the entire world apart. Awaking alone within the unfamiliar wilderness of another plane of existence, Qhira was forced to adapt and survive in a hostile and far more primitive new world. Now, years later, she has established herself as a mercenary... a bounty hunter: willing to take any job she deems fit. This is the side she shows the world, an efficient and calculating hunter that can surmount any challenge. Yet in truth, she is hiding away a pain that constantly eats away at her... a desperate desire to find other survivors of her world's destruction. And she is willing to pay any price to do so.
